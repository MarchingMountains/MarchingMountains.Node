Welcome Band Directors

If you are a band director of a public school band program in Appalachia or Minnesota, please log in on the top right, then click here to register your school to receive donations.

If you have any questions, please contact me, Ian Felton, founder, at donate@marchingmountains.org. We have provided over 500 years of band to students who would otherwise not be able to participate by donating instruments in good working condition to band programs in need.
